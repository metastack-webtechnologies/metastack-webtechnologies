Centralized Order Hub

Pain Point: Juggling multiple tablets for Swiggy, Zomato, and your own website leads to missed orders, mistakes, and a stressed-out front-of-house team.

An Order Hub is the heart of a modern restaurant. It consolidates every single order—from third-party delivery apps, your website, QR code table orders, and even phone calls—into one single, intuitive screen.

The MetaStack Solution

We build a custom dashboard that provides a bird's-eye view of your entire operation. It's more than just a list; it's an intelligent command center.

A single, clean interface for all your orders.

Smart Kitchen Display System (KDS)

Pain Point: Paper tickets get lost, are hard to read, and create chaos in the kitchen. This leads to incorrect orders and slow preparation times.

A KDS replaces traditional paper printers with digital screens. Orders from the Order Hub appear instantly on screens in the kitchen, organized by priority and station.

Digital tickets, organized by station and priority.

The MetaStack Solution

Our KDS is designed for speed and clarity. It routes orders to the correct stations, tracks prep times, and allows cooks to mark items as complete with a simple tap, notifying the front-of-house automatically.

Dynamic Digital Menus

Pain Point: Reprinting menus to change one price or mark an item as "sold out" is expensive and slow. Updating menus on Zomato, Swiggy, and your website individually is a tedious chore.

A centralized menu management system allows you to update a dish, change a price, or add a special from one place and have it reflect instantly across all your platforms: in-house QR menus, your website, and third-party apps.

Update once, publish everywhere.

The MetaStack Solution

We provide a simple interface where you can manage your entire menu. Mark an item "out of stock" and it's instantly unavailable for order everywhere, preventing customer disappointment. Run a "happy hour" special with just a few clicks.

Integrated Point of Sale (POS)

Pain Point: Traditional POS systems are clunky, disconnected from online orders, and make tasks like splitting bills or applying discounts a nightmare.

A modern, integrated POS is the cash register, billing system, and payment processor all in one. It's directly connected to your Order Hub and KDS, meaning every order, whether dine-in or delivery, is tracked in your sales data automatically.

A fully connected system.

The MetaStack Solution

Our POS solutions run on any tablet or dedicated device. They are designed for speed and ease of use, with features like one-tap bill splitting, easy discount application, and integration with modern payment gateways like UPI and card machines.

Customer Relationship Management (CRM)

Pain Point: You have repeat customers, but you don't know who they are, what they like, or how to encourage them to come back more often.

A restaurant CRM builds a profile for every customer. It tracks their order history, favorite dishes, visit frequency, and total spending. This data is gold for building loyalty and personalizing their experience.

Understand and reward your best customers.

The MetaStack Solution

We integrate a CRM that automatically captures customer data from online orders and reservations. Use this to run automated loyalty programs (e.g., "Your 10th order is on us!") or send targeted marketing campaigns via SMS or email ("We know you love our Biryani, get 15% off today!").
