Core Components of a Clinic OS

Online Appointment Booking

Pain Point: Time-consuming phone calls and scheduling errors.

Solution: A 24/7 online portal for patients to book their own appointments. Reduces front-desk work and eliminates double bookings.

Digital Patient Records (DPR)

Pain Point: Insecure, disorganized, and hard-to-access paper files.

Solution: A secure, centralized system to store and retrieve patient history, notes, and documents instantly and from anywhere.

Automated Communication

Pain Point: Manual reminders are inefficient; no-shows cause revenue loss.

Solution: Automatic SMS/Email reminders and follow-ups. Reduces no-shows and improves patient engagement with no manual effort.

Simple Billing & Invoicing

Pain Point: Billing is a manual, repetitive, and error-prone task.

Solution: One-click generation of professional bills and receipts. Integrates with patient records for accuracy and efficiency.
